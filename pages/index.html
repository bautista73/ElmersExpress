Our Services

Residential Moving

We specialize in providing comprehensive moving services for individuals and families. Our experienced team will handle all aspects of your move, including loading, unloading, and arranging your belongings with speed and efficiency.

Office Relocation &
Commercial Moving

Commercial and office relocations can be complex and time-consuming, that's why we offer professional relocation services to streamline the process and ensure a seamless move. Our team will handle all aspects of the move, including loading, unloading, and arranging office equipment and furnishings with speed and efficiency.

Warehousing & Receiving

Whether you need short-term or reciving services, our flexible solutions are designed to meet your specific requirements. Our team of professionals will handle the loading and unloading of your items with care, and provide you with updates on the status of your goods.

Delivery Services

We understand the importance of timely delivery, which is why we prioritize punctuality and customer satisfaction. Choose us for your next delivery and experience the peace of mind that comes with working with a trusted and dependable delivery company. Contact us today to learn more about our delivery services.

Assisted Living &
Nursing Home Moving

We understand that moving can be a difficult and emotional experience, especially for residents of assisted living and nursing homes. That's why we take extra care when handling their belongings and work to minimize any disruption to their daily routine. Our team will work closely with staff to ensure a seamless and smooth transition for the residents.

Packing &
Model Home Moving

Whether you're relocating a model home to a new location or simply rearranging the existing space, our team will carefully wrap and secure each item to ensure it arrives in the same condition it was in before the move. With our tailored solution and attention to detail, you can trust us to handle your model home moving and packing needs with the utmost care.

Hourly Labor

Optimize the pace and productivity of your relocation (or home project) with our team to handle loading, unloading, or rearranging your belongings. Whether you require assistance with filling a truck or portable storage unit, or preparing for an event, we have the solution for you.

Floor Moves &
Home Show Events

Whether you're moving furniture, equipment, or decor for a floor move or setting up for a home show event, our team will work closely with you to ensure that your needs are met. We understand the importance of minimizing downtime and ensuring a seamless transition, which is why we work quickly and efficiently to get the job done right.

About Us

At Elmer's Express Delivery, we offer a complete transportation solution. Moving your items quickly, affordably, and safely from point A to point B.

Our fully licensed and insured operations are regulated by the Washington Utilities and Transportation Commission (WUTC), ensuring liability and cargo protection.

As a family-owned and operated business, we are dedicated to providing excellent service at a fair price while upholding our core values of respect, honesty, and hard work.
